Brief and approach

Renaissance Sports Group engaged me to promote the launch of the Erima range, targeting club tenders across Australia. Working within Erima’s established German brand identity, I developed social and promotional assets to engage local clubs, build early traction and support the tender process, using a clear and repeatable system that highlighted product features and drove action.
