A little bit
about me

I grew up in Mildura before moving to Melbourne in 2019 to follow my passion for the creative arts. I quickly discovered that graphic design’s blend of creativity and practical problem-solving was exactly what I’d been looking for and landed my first role as a junior designer with a sports-tech collective. After completing a Diploma of Graphic Design, I developed a newfound love of illustration. Now I freelance from home but still crave the camaraderie of a creative team.

When I’m off the clock, you’ll often find me sketching ideas or tweaking my website over a bowl of cereal. On weekends I’m happiest trail-running, road-tripping, camping riverside, or at the pub with my mates.
